About VANTAGE Running

VANTAGE Running is a performance apparel brand built by runners, based in the Hutt Valley — Te Awa Kairangi, New Zealand.

We started the brand because we wanted gear that was worth wearing. Performance fabric, clean design, made locally, and priced without the markup that comes with a big label.

Between track, cross country, and the trails out our back door, we've worn a lot of running kit — and we know the difference.

We're starting with shirts, but VANTAGE is built to grow. More products are coming, all held to the same standard: perform well, look good, made here.

Everything is printed in Lower Hutt and made with materials we trust. We use AS Colour blanks — known for their ethical supply chain and quality fabric — because the standard of what goes into our apparel matters as much as what goes on it.
